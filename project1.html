Modern Residential Complex

Contemporary living spaces designed for modern urban life

Location

Downtown Portland, Oregon

Year

2023

Size

45,000 sq ft

Units

32 Residential Units

Project Overview

This modern residential complex represents a new approach to urban living, combining contemporary design with sustainable building practices. The 32-unit development features clean lines, expansive windows, and thoughtfully designed communal spaces that foster community interaction.

The building's facade utilizes a mix of natural materials including locally sourced timber and stone, creating a warm contrast to the sleek metal and glass elements. Each unit is designed to maximize natural light while maintaining privacy through strategic placement of windows and balconies.

Design Philosophy

The design prioritizes both individual comfort and community connection. Ground-floor retail spaces activate the street level, while rooftop gardens and shared terraces provide residents with outdoor amenities and stunning city views.
