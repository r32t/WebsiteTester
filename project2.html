Cultural Center

A dynamic community gathering space celebrating arts and culture

Location

Seattle, Washington

Year

2024

Size

28,000 sq ft

Capacity

500 People

Project Overview

The Cultural Center serves as a vibrant hub for community arts programming, featuring flexible performance spaces, artist studios, and exhibition galleries. The design emphasizes transparency and accessibility, with large glass facades that invite the community to engage with the activities inside.

The building's distinctive angular form creates dynamic interior spaces while responding to the urban context. A central atrium serves as the heart of the building, connecting all levels and providing a dramatic backdrop for performances and events.

Sustainable Features

The project incorporates numerous sustainable design elements including a green roof system, rainwater collection, and high-performance glazing. Natural ventilation strategies reduce energy consumption while maintaining optimal comfort for performances and exhibitions.
